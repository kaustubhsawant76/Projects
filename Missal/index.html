The dish originates from the boundary of Khandesh and Western Maharashtra. Present day Nasik and Ahmednagar districts of Maharashtra The origin of Misal Pav is not entirely clear, but it is believed to have been invented by a local Maratha warrior named Kondaji Farzand. in year 1985
